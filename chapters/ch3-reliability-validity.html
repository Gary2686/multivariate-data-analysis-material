信度與效度：基本概念與衡量

Ch.1 測量誤差 + Ch.3 總加量表之信效度評估

一、為什麼信度與效度很重要？

在多變量分析中，我們依賴變數的組合（variate）來進行估計與推論。但所有觀察到的變數都包含某種程度的測量誤差（measurement error）。

測量誤差會在觀察值中加入「雜訊」（noise），使得：

  • 相關係數被低估（弱化真實關係）
  • 平均數不精確
  • 多變量模型的統計檢力下降

In multivariate analysis, we rely on combinations of variables (variates) for estimation and inference. But all observed variables contain some degree of measurement error.

Measurement error adds "noise" to observed variables, causing:

  • Correlations to be weakened (masking true relationships)
  • Means to be less precise
  • Reduced statistical power in multivariate models

核心公式：觀察值 = 真實值 + 測量誤差

Xobserved = Xtrue + eerror 減少測量誤差是研究者的首要目標之一

解決方案：多重衡量（Multivariate Measurement）

使用多個指標（indicators）來衡量同一概念，而非僅依賴單一問項。多個回應的「平均值」更能反映真實值，這就是總加量表（summated scales）的核心思維。

Use multiple indicators to measure the same concept rather than relying on a single item. The "average" across related responses more accurately reflects the true value — this is the rationale behind summated scales.

二、效度（Validity）

效度是指測量工具精確代表所欲衡量概念的程度。即使測量非常精確，如果問的是「錯誤的問題」，仍然無效。

例如：想衡量「可支配收入」，卻問「家庭總收入」→ 精確但無效。

Validity is the degree to which a measure accurately represents what it is supposed to. Even if measurement is precise, asking the "wrong question" makes it invalid.

Example: Wanting to measure "discretionary income" but asking about "total household income" → precise but invalid.

效度的四種類型

1. 內容效度 / 表面效度

定義：量表項目與概念定義之間的對應程度。

評估方式：透過專家判斷、前測、多個子群體檢驗等主觀方式進行評估。

重要性：這是最基本的效度，必須在任何統計測試之前先確認。

Content validity assesses whether items match the conceptual definition through expert ratings and pretests.

Definition: The correspondence between individual items and the conceptual definition of the construct.

Assessment: Subjective evaluation through expert judges, pretests, and multiple subpopulations.

Importance: The most fundamental validity; must be established before any statistical testing.

2. 聚合效度（Convergent Validity）

定義：衡量同一概念的兩個或多個指標之間高度相關的程度。

評估方式：

  • 因素負荷量（Factor loadings）應 ≥ .50，理想 ≥ .70
  • 平均變異萃取量（AVE）應 ≥ .50
  • 組合信度（CR）應 ≥ .70

High correlations between measures of the same concept indicate convergent validity.

Definition: The degree to which two or more measures of the same concept are highly correlated.

Assessment:

  • Factor loadings should be ≥ .50, ideally ≥ .70
  • Average Variance Extracted (AVE) should be ≥ .50
  • Composite Reliability (CR) should be ≥ .70

3. 區辨效度（Discriminant Validity）

定義：兩個概念上相似但理論上不同的概念之間，確實存在差異的程度。

評估方式：

  • 量表與相似但不同的概念之間的相關應該低
  • Fornell-Larcker判準：AVE > 兩建構間相關的平方

Measures should be sufficiently different from conceptually similar but distinct constructs.

Definition: The degree to which two conceptually similar concepts are truly distinct.

Assessment:

  • Correlation with similar but distinct measures should be low
  • Fornell-Larcker criterion: AVE > squared correlation between constructs

4. 法則效度（Nomological Validity）

定義：量表在理論模型中能準確預測其他概念的程度。

評估方式：檢驗量表是否展現出理論或過去研究所支持的關係模式。

邏輯：如果理論預測 A → B，而我們的量表所測得的 A 確實與 B 有預期的關聯，則具備法則效度。

Tests whether the scale demonstrates theoretically supported relationships with other concepts.

Definition: The degree to which the scale makes accurate predictions of other concepts in a theoretically-based model.

Assessment: Examine whether the scale demonstrates relationships that theory or prior research supports.

Logic: If theory predicts A → B, and our measure of A does relate to B as expected, nomological validity is supported.

效度類型 核心問題 評估方法 期望結果
內容/表面效度 項目是否符合概念定義？ 專家判斷、前測 主觀一致性
聚合效度 相同概念的指標是否相關？ 因素負荷量、AVE、CR 高相關 / 高負荷量
區辨效度 不同概念是否真正不同？ AVE vs. r², 模型比較 低跨概念相關
法則效度 量表能否如理論預測？ 理論關係驗證 符合理論預期

三、信度（Reliability）

信度是指觀察變數衡量「真實值」且「無誤差」的程度。它關心的不是「我們是否測了對的東西」（那是效度的問題），而是「我們測量的方式是否一致」。

信度與效度的關係：有效度必然有信度，但有信度不一定有效度。

Reliability is the degree to which the observed variable measures the "true" value and is "error free." It concerns not "whether we measured the right thing" (that's validity) but "whether our measurement is consistent."

The relationship: Validity implies reliability, but reliability does not imply validity.

再測信度（Test-Retest Reliability）

在兩個不同時間點對同一群受試者進行相同的測量，比較兩次結果的一致性。

  • 目的：確保測量結果不會因時間而產生過大的變異
  • 評估：計算兩次測量的相關係數
  • 限制：受測者可能因記憶效果或練習效果而影響結果

Test-Retest Reliability

Measures consistency between responses of the same individuals at two points in time.

  • Purpose: Ensure responses are not too varied across time periods
  • Assessment: Correlation between the two measurements
  • Limitation: Memory or practice effects may influence results

內部一致性（Internal Consistency）— Cronbach's Alpha

最常用的信度指標。評估量表中各題項之間的一致性程度。

Cronbach's α = (k / (k-1)) × (1 - Σσ²i / σ²total) k = 題項數, σ²i = 各題項變異數, σ²total = 總分變異數

判斷標準（Hair et al.）：

  • α ≥ .70：良好，可接受的基本門檻
  • α ≥ .60：僅在探索性研究中可接受
  • α < .60：信度不足，需要改善量表
  • 題項數越多（接近10題以上），門檻值應提高

Internal Consistency — Cronbach's Alpha

The most commonly used reliability measure. Assesses consistency among variables in a summated scale.

Cronbach's α = (k / (k-1)) × (1 - Σσ²i / σ²total) k = number of items, σ²i = item variances, σ²total = total score variance

Thresholds (Hair et al.):

  • α ≥ .70: Good, basic acceptable threshold
  • α ≥ .60: Acceptable only in exploratory research
  • α < .60: Insufficient reliability, scale needs improvement
  • Threshold should be raised as items approach 10 or more

進階診斷工具

項目-總分相關（Item-to-total correlation）：每個題項與總分之間的相關。低相關的題項可能需要刪除。

刪除後的 Alpha（Alpha if item deleted）：如果刪除某題後 Alpha 值上升，代表該題項可能降低了量表信度。

項目間相關（Inter-item correlations）：每對題項之間的相關係數。應該都是正向且適度的。

Item-to-total correlation: Correlation of each item with the summated scale score. Items with low correlations may need to be deleted.

Alpha if item deleted: If Alpha increases when an item is removed, that item may be reducing scale reliability.

Inter-item correlations: Correlations between every pair of items. Should all be positive and moderate.

組合信度（Composite Reliability, CR）

在 SEM / CFA 中更常使用的信度指標，基於因素負荷量計算。

CR = (Σλi)² / [(Σλi)² + Σei] λi = 標準化因素負荷量, ei = 誤差變異 (1 - λ²i)

判斷標準：CR ≥ .70 表示具有足夠的內部一致性。

與 Cronbach's α 的差異：CR 考慮了不同題項的因素負荷量差異（加權計算），而 α 假設所有題項權重相等。因此 CR 通常被認為較 α 更為準確。

Composite Reliability (CR)

More commonly used in SEM/CFA contexts, calculated from factor loadings.

CR = (Σλi)² / [(Σλi)² + Σei] λi = standardized factor loading, ei = error variance (1 - λ²i)

Threshold: CR ≥ .70 indicates adequate internal consistency.

Difference from Cronbach's α: CR accounts for different factor loadings (weighted), while α assumes equal weights. Thus CR is generally considered more accurate.

重要提醒（Rules of Thumb 3-7）

  • 量表再好也只取決於所用的題項品質——即使通過所有統計檢驗，沒有理論基礎仍然無用
  • 在評估信度之前，必須先以 EFA 或 CFA 確認單一維度性
  • Cronbach's α 應至少達到 .70（探索性研究可放寬到 .60）
  • 確認信度後，才能進一步評估效度
  • A scale is only as good as its items — even if it passes all empirical tests, it is useless without theoretical justification
  • Must first confirm unidimensionality with EFA or CFA before assessing reliability
  • Cronbach's α should exceed .70 (.60 acceptable for exploratory research)
  • Once reliability is established, then assess validity

八、關鍵名詞解釋

測量誤差（Measurement Error）

觀察值未能代表「真實值」的程度。來源包括資料輸入錯誤、量表精度不足（如使用7點量表但受試者只能區分3個等級），以及受試者無法精確提供資訊。測量誤差會弱化相關係數並降低統計檢力。

The degree to which observed values are not representative of the "true" values. Sources include data entry errors, imprecision of measurement, and inability of respondents to accurately provide information. It weakens correlations and reduces statistical power.

總加量表（Summated Scale）

將多個衡量同一概念的變數合併為一個單一的複合指標，通常使用平均分數。好處包括：(1) 降低測量誤差——多個指標減少對單一回應的依賴；(2) 在單一指標中代表概念的多個面向。

Combining several variables measuring the same concept into a single composite measure, usually using the average score. Benefits: (1) Reduces measurement error by not relying on a single response; (2) Represents multiple aspects of a concept in a single measure.

單一維度性（Unidimensionality）

量表中的所有題項都強烈關聯且僅代表一個單一概念。這是建構總加量表的基本假設與必要條件。透過因素分析檢驗——所有題項應高度負荷在同一個因素上。若量表被提出具有多個維度，每個維度應被一個獨立的因素所反映。

All items are strongly associated and represent a single concept. This is a fundamental assumption and requirement for summated scales. Tested through factor analysis — all items should load highly on a single factor. If a scale has multiple dimensions, each should be reflected by a separate factor.

平均變異萃取量 AVE（Average Variance Extracted）

代表一組題項中被其潛在建構所解釋的平均變異比例。計算方式為所有標準化因素負荷量的平方之平均值。

AVE = Σλ²ᵢ / n

AVE ≥ .50 表示建構解釋了其指標至少一半以上的變異，支持聚合效度。AVE < .50 表示平均而言，誤差變異大於被建構所解釋的變異。

The average percentage of variation explained among the items of a construct. Calculated as the mean of all squared standardized factor loadings.

AVE = Σλ²ᵢ / n

AVE ≥ .50 means the construct explains at least half the variance of its indicators, supporting convergent validity. AVE < .50 means more error remains than explained variance on average.

反向計分（Reverse Scoring）

當同一因素中有正向與反向負荷的變數時，必須對反向負荷的題項進行反向計分，使所有負荷都為正向。例如在0-10的量表上：反向計分 = 10 - 原始分數。這確保所有題項的方向一致後才能計算總加量表分數。

When variables within the same factor have both positive and negative loadings, the negatively loaded items must be reverse scored so all loadings become positive. For example, on a 0-10 scale: reverse score = 10 - original value. This ensures consistent direction before computing summated scale scores.
